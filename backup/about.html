Why we built this.

It didn't start with a problem. It started with a person.

A creator who waited four months to be paid for a campaign she delivered in two days. An agency founder who spent his 30th birthday reconciling TDS certificates for 28 creators instead of celebrating. A brand marketing head who defended a ₹12 lakh campaign spend to her CEO with a PDF of screenshots and a prayer.

These aren't hypotheticals. These are the people Khi Khi was built for. The platform exists because the current system fails all three of them—simultaneously, repeatedly, and unnecessarily.

Our Mission

"To make the creator economy work—for real. Where every creator is a business, every brand knows exactly what their rupee is doing, and every transaction is transparent by default."

Why India, Why Now

900M Internet Users

India is at the epicenter of the global creator surge, with 3.5 to 4.5 million active creators building the new digital economy.

The Infrastructure Gap

What we're working toward

India first. APAC second. The compliance and escrow infrastructure eventually licensed as a B2B layer for platforms globally.

Khi Khi becomes the financial and trust infrastructure of the global creator economy.
