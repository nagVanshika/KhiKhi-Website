The Khi Khi Promise

"We built Khi Khi because we were tired of an industry that runs on trust while doing nothing to earn it."

We will never hide a rupee.

Every fee, every tax, every deduction is visible before anything moves.

We make our money from a transparent platform fee. We don't take a cut of creator payments and call it something else. We don't add charges after the fact. What you see is exactly what happens.

We will never let a creator go unpaid for work they've done.

The escrow exists because "trust us, the payment is coming" is not good enough.

The moment a campaign is created, the money is locked. The creator knows it. The brand knows it. There is no version of events where a creator delivers and doesn't get paid.

We will never show you data we can't verify.

The Instagram API connection is not a convenience—it's a commitment.

We will not show you metrics that came from a screenshot, a scraper, or a creator typing numbers into a form. Every stat you see on Khi Khi was pulled directly from Instagram's official API. If we can't verify it, we don't show it.

We will handle your compliance so you never have to worry about it again.

GST, TDS, PAN verification, KYC, CIN checks—all of it automated, all of it accurate.

Not as an add-on. Built into every transaction, always. Your financial safety and compliance are our baseline, not a feature.

"The creator economy is worth ₹33,750 crore and growing at 22% a year. It deserves infrastructure that's worthy of it. That's what we're building."
